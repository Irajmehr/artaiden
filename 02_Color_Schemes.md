## Usage Guidelines

### How to Use These Color Schemes

1. **Choose Based on Your Brand Identity**: Select a palette that aligns with your brand personality and industry
2. **Consider Your Audience**: Different colors evoke different emotions - choose accordingly
3. **Test Readability**: Always ensure sufficient contrast between background and text colors
4. **Be Consistent**: Use the same palette across your entire website for cohesive branding
5. **Use the 60-30-10 Rule**: 60% primary color, 30% secondary color, 10% accent color

### Accessibility Considerations

- All font color recommendations follow WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards for color contrast
- White text on dark backgrounds: Minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1
- Dark text on light backgrounds: Minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1
- For large text (18pt+ or 14pt bold+): Minimum contrast ratio of 3:1

### Color Psychology Quick Reference

- **Blue**: Trust, professionalism, calm, stability
- **Green**: Growth, health, nature, harmony
- **Red**: Energy, passion, urgency, excitement
- **Orange**: Friendliness, confidence, creativity
- **Yellow**: Optimism, clarity, warmth
- **Purple**: Luxury, creativity, wisdom
- **Pink**: Playfulness, compassion, sophistication
- **Black**: Power, elegance, sophistication
- **White**: Purity, simplicity, cleanliness
- **Gray**: Neutrality, balance, professionalism

### Implementation Tips

1. **Start with Primary Colors**: Use these for headers, major UI elements, and backgrounds
2. **Secondary Colors for Accents**: Use for hover states, borders, and supporting elements
3. **Maintain Hierarchy**: Ensure your color choices guide users naturally through your content
4. **Test on Multiple Devices**: Colors may appear differently on various screens
5. **Consider Cultural Context**: Colors have different meanings in different cultures

### Tools Integration

These color schemes can be easily imported into:
- Design tools (Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch)
- Development environments (CSS variables, Tailwind config, SASS variables)
- Brand guidelines and design systems
- Marketing materials and templates
